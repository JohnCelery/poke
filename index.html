How to Play

  1. Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your trainer around the map.
  2. Explore the tall grass to trigger wild encounters.
  3. In battle choose from Attack, Special, Heal, or Run.
  4. Defeat wild creatures to earn experience and level up your party.

Tip: Special attacks are powerful but cost more energy. Heal restores some health.
